The Rooms

Sweetness of living

Spacious and comfortable, the rooms and bathrooms invite serenity and the pleasure of sharing, while offering each guest their own private space. Each room has its own character, reflecting a unique harmony between authenticity and refinement. Noble materials, traditional masonry, oak finishes... Here, every detail has been thought out to infuse a touch of soul and transform your stay into an unforgettable experience. Let yourself be carried away, push the door... and enter a place where you feel good, simply.
